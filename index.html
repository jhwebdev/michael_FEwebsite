Fire Emblem is a long-running game series that involves turn-based strategy with a medevil-esque setting that touches upon the subject of politics and war. It's debut game started in 1990 and is still going strong presently, with many iterations in the games and has several other spin-offs, a mobile game, manga, and very short anime series. It's a tactical JRPG game with lots of variables making each playthrough different in some way or another, creating a replayability through all the games. On every page there will be a showcase of the lords of each game, starting with Marth, Alm, and Seliph (up to down).
